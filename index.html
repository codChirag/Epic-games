About this Demo Project

This page is long intentionally: it contains documentation, developer notes, accessibility considerations, and sample JSON game data. Use it to explain your project during submission or viva.

Developer Notes

  1. Architecture: Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS — no frameworks so you can explain every line.
  2. State: localStorage for cart & language preference.
  3. Images: Use your own or royalty-free images placed in assets/.

Accessibility

Aria attributes, keyboard-accessible controls, and focus outlines are included. You can expand on these in your report.
